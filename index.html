How to Use Green Fingers Fertilizer

Proper usage of Green Fingers Fertilizer is essential to maximize its benefits and promote healthy plant growth. Follow these simple steps to ensure the effective application of our fertilizer:

  • Measure: Determine the amount of fertilizer needed based on the size of your watering can or sprayer. For every 10 liters of water, use 25ml of Green Fingers Fertilizer. This balanced ratio ensures the optimal concentration of nutrients for your plants.
  • Mix: Pour the appropriate amount of Green Finger Fertilizer into the watering can or sprayer. Then, add water gradually while stirring or shaking gently to ensure thorough mixing. This will help distribute the nutrients evenly throughout the solution.
  • Watering: Use the prepared Green Fingers Fertilizer solution to water the base of your plants. Apply the fertilizer solution evenly, making sure to cover the entire root zone. Avoid applying the fertilizer directly to the leaves, as it may cause burns or damage.
  • Frequency: Apply Green Fingers Fertilizer once a week during the growing season. Consistent and regular application ensures a continuous supply of essential nutrients, supporting healthy growth and development.
  • Observation: Observe your plants closely after applying Green Fingers Fertilizer. Look for signs of improved vitality, such as enhanced foliage color, increased flowering, and overall healthier plant appearance. Regular usage of our fertilizer can lead to stronger, more resilient plants.
  • Storage: Store Green Fingers Fertilizer in a cool and dry place, away from direct sunlight. Ensure the bottle is tightly sealed to maintain its quality and effectiveness. Follow the recommended shelf-life guidelines mentioned on the packaging.

Remember, every plant has unique needs, so it's essential to consider specific plant requirements, including sunlight, water, and other environmental factors. Green Fingers Fertilizer works in harmony with these factors to provide the necessary nutrients for optimal plant growth. If you have any specific questions or concerns about the usage of Green Fingers Fertilizer, feel free to reach out to our knowledgeable team for guidance and support.

How Does Green Fingers Fertilizer Work?

Green Fingers Fertilizer is designed to provide your plants with the essential nutrients they need for healthy growth and development. Here's how our fertilizer works:

  • Balanced Nutrient Blend: Green Fingers Fertilizer contains a unique blend of 14 essential elements that are crucial for plant growth. These elements include nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), potassium (K), and various micronutrients such as iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), and zinc (Zn). The balanced composition ensures that your plants receive a comprehensive array of nutrients.
  • Nutrient Uptake: The nutrients in Green Finger Fertilizer are in a form that is readily available and easily absorbed by plants. When applied to the soil or sprayed on the leaves, the fertilizer is taken up by the roots or leaves and transported throughout the plant.
  • Plant Growth Stimulation: The carefully selected blend of nutrients in Green Fingers Fertilizer stimulates plant growth in multiple ways. Nitrogen promotes leaf and stem development, phosphorus supports root growth and flowering, and potassium enhances overall plant health and disease resistance.
  • Root Development: The nutrients in Green Finger Fertilizer play a vital role in root development. Strong and healthy roots are essential for efficient nutrient absorption and water uptake. By providing the necessary nutrients, our fertilizer promotes robust root growth, enabling plants to access nutrients and moisture more effectively.
  • Enhanced Nutrient Utilization: Green Fingers Fertilizer optimizes the utilization of nutrients within the plant. The presence of micronutrients ensures that plants can efficiently utilize macronutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. This leads to improved nutrient absorption and utilization, maximizing the benefits for your plants.
  • Healthy Plant Functions: The balanced nutrient supply from Green Fingers Fertilizer supports various plant functions. It aids in photosynthesis, energy production, enzyme activation, and the synthesis of proteins and other essential compounds. As a result, plants thrive and exhibit vibrant growth, increased flower production, and overall vigor.
  • Environmental-Friendly Formulation: At Ayushman Organics, we prioritize the use of eco-friendly and sustainable practices. Green Fingers Fertilizer is formulated with natural and organic ingredients whenever possible, minimizing the environmental impact and ensuring safe usage for your plants, soil, and surrounding ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about Green Fingers Fertilizer:

  • Using Green Fingers Fertilizer only, sufficient for our plants?

    Yes, it contains all the essential elements that are necessary for plant growth and are often depleted from the soil.

  • Can we use more than the recommended quantity?

    Using more than the recommended quantity will not be beneficial, as the soil will only absorb the required elements and the excess will be washed out.

  • How often can we use it?

    You can use it every time you water your plants, but keep in mind that plants and soil will only absorb what they need. We recommend using it once a week.

  • Will it damage the soil?

    Using the recommended quantity will not have any damaging effect on the soil or microorganisms. In fact, the microflora will thrive as the plants thrive.

  • What is the recommended quantity?

    The recommended quantity is 2.5 ml per 1 liter of water, or 25 ml for 10 liters of water, and so on, once a week as watering.

  • Why should we dilute it with water before applying?

    The solution is in concentrate form, and plants cannot absorb it without diluting it.

  • What is the benefit of giving it as watering the plants?

    Applying the fertilizer as watering ensures that it reaches to every root of the plants, allowing the plants to absorb the fertilizer along with water.

  • Can we use it for foliar spray?

    Yes, it can be used as a foliar spray by adding 2.5 ml of the solution to 1 liter of water and spraying it on the leaves.

  • Can we add anything else to the plants along with Green Fingers Fertilizer?

    Yes, you can add curd or buttermilk with the fertilizer.

  • How long will it take for my plants to react?

    It could take from 14 to 28 days after the first application and regular use of Fertilizer.

  • What elements it contains?

    It contains Nitrogen, Potassium, Phosphorus, Magnesium, Calcium, Iron, Zinc, Sulphur, Copper, Manganese, Boron, Nickel, Molybdenum, and Chlorine.
